About The Auther

Eloisa B. Lucas


Eloisa Alvarez Bonifacio is married to Pablo M. Lucas. She holds a bachelor's degree in business administration (BBA), is a certified public accountant (CPA, Philippines), a certified government financial manager (CGFM, U.S.A.), a certified fraud specialist (CFS, U.S.A.), and a current tax and financial consultant of EBL & ASSOCIATES (San Diego, CA, U.S.A.), a tax consulting office she established since 1988.

She has borne six children and has seven grandchildren, and she is still active in her profession. Eloisa was the first woman accountant sent by Citizens Democracy Corps (CDC, a nonprofit corporation who has contributed immensely to USAID'S development efforts) to Ukraine, its Villages and Cities, and the Crimean Region in 1997 to present a seminar to the accounting teachers on the subject of Western and International Accounting.

In 1998, she helped some business people in the former USSR Republic of Georgia in their understanding of the western accounting system. She is a member and in the directory of International Who's Who of Professionals (1998), Who's Who of the New Millennium 2000 Special Edition, and Marquis Who's Who Directory 2000-02.
